Salt Lake City, USA

Salt Lake City is the capital and largest city of Utah, known for its stunning mountain scenery and outdoor recreation opportunities. It was founded in 1847 by Mormon pioneers and is famous for the nearby Great Salt Lake. The city is a hub for culture, business, and education, home to the University of Utah, vibrant arts, and historic landmarks. Salt Lake City also hosted the 2002 Winter Olympics, showcasing its world-class winter sports facilities.
